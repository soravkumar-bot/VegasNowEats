Insider Tips

Eat Smart in Vegas

🕐

Eat Before 6pm

Many Strip restaurants offer early dining specials and pre-theatre menus at 30–40% off. Get in before the rush.

📍

Leave the Strip

Chinatown is a 10-minute Lyft from the casino. Better food, lower prices, and zero tourist markup.

🍱

Lunch = Better Value

Many premium restaurants offer lunch menus using the same kitchen as dinner — at half the price.

🎟

Book Ahead for Big Names

Gordon Ramsay, Nobu, é by José Andrés — these fill up weeks in advance. Don't show up and hope.

🌙

Embrace the Late Night

Vegas's best secret: incredible food between midnight and 4am if you know the right spots.

💳

Casino Rewards = Food Credit

If you're playing at all, sign up for players clubs — many convert points into dining credits at on-property restaurants.
